Prototype Co-Pilot:
a PM workflow, automated.

Four AI-powered Claude Code skills that chain together into a complete prototype design pipeline. Research to deliverables in a single session.

⚙ Automated workflow 🔍 Deep research & Five Whys 🛑 Human-in-the-loop checkpoints
💻 View on GitHub →
Step 01
🔍
/pm-research

Deep-dive into any product: features, App Store reviews, competitors, UI patterns — automatically integrating Firecrawl, Claude, and web access tools.

Output: research-brief.md
Step 02
🎯
/pm-design

Synthesize pain points, build behavioral frameworks, define personas with goal-first architecture — continuously digging into root causes via Five Whys analysis.

Output: design-spec.md + persona.md
Step 03
⚡
/pm-build

Generate interactive React/JSX prototypes with iPhone framing. Zero-build, browser-ready demos — user stays in control at every checkpoint.

Output: preview.html (live prototype)
Step 04
📦
/pm-package

Auto-generate stakeholder emails, blog posts, and Lovable.dev build prompts from your prototype.

Output: deliverables/ (email + blog + prompt)
What makes this workflow different
01
Automated Tool Orchestration
One command triggers a coordinated research session across Firecrawl, Claude Code, web search, screenshot extraction — every tool working together.
02
Deep Research & Five Whys
Never stops at surface data. Keeps digging into root causes — pulling user reviews, competitor UIs, behavioral patterns — until the real problem is uncovered.
03
Human-in-the-Loop Checkpoints
Key decisions stay with you. The workflow pauses at critical points — you authorize plans, approve designs, inject your own judgment. No black boxes.

🏋️ Health & Fitness × AI Agent

WHOOP AI Coach

A personalized AI wellness agent that transforms WHOOP's biometric data into daily action plans. Instead of just showing "recovery 52%," it tells you exactly what to do, when, and why — adapted to your specific goals and body patterns.

Design Principles

Goals First Every screen element connects to the user's stated goal. Not features-first — outcomes-first.
6-Stage Loop Set Goal → Morning Briefing → Active Coaching → Logging → Evening Review → Adaptation
Personalized Coach Three AI personalities matched to user type: Aria (optimizer), Marcus (athlete), Zen (executive).
Close the Loop Most apps give data. This coach tracks adherence, correlates behaviors with outcomes, and learns what works for YOU.

Featured Persona: Sarah

Sarah, 28 — Marketing manager training for her first marathon. Goal: "Complete marathon injury-free while improving my time."

Coach Marcus guides her with patience and injury-prevention focus: "52% recovery means easy miles today. Trust the process — this is how we stay healthy for race day."
